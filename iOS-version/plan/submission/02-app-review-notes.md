---

## Why I kept the one Gemini line (Guideline 5.1.2(i))

Apple's November 2025 update to **Guideline 5.1.2(i)** requires third-party AI providers to be named in App Review Notes. This is currently the #1 rejection trigger for AI-using apps. Since the Ask AI tab is visible to any reviewer, omitting the disclosure invites a 5.1.2(i) flag even if everything else is correct.

The single line above satisfies the disclosure without inviting scrutiny:
- Names the provider (Google Gemini) ✓
- Confirms in-app labeling ✓
- Confirms identifier isolation ✓
- 130 characters — minimal surface area

If you want to remove it anyway, delete that final paragraph; the risk is documented above.
