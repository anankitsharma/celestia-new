## 2. What Celestia is

Celestia is a personal-reflection app. It uses your birth-chart data — the planetary positions for your time and place of birth — as the input to a written-reflection layer that helps you think about yourself, your patterns, and your relationships.

The reflections are produced by combining:

- An **astronomical engine** that calculates planetary positions (the open-source `astronomy-engine` library).
- A **traditional Western astrology** reasoning layer that turns those positions into themes.
- A **generative AI** layer (Google Gemini) that turns those themes into written prompts and replies.

Celestia is intended for **entertainment, reflection, and self-development**. It is not a substitute for professional advice.
