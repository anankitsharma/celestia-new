## Why the Apple-specific clause (Section 17)?

Required by Apple's [Schedule 2](https://developer.apple.com/support/standard-license-template/) for apps using Apple's standard EULA. If you opt to use a custom EULA in App Store Connect, this clause makes your custom EULA Apple-compliant.

In App Store Connect:
- **App Information → License Agreement** → leave at "Standard EULA" if comfortable. Otherwise upload your custom Terms.

## Why "Not advice" prominently in Section 3?

Because the app uses generative AI to comment on relationships and personal questions, this is the disclaimer that protects against:
- 2.3.1 (misleading functionality)
- 5.5 (mobile fortune-telling concerns)
- General product-liability risk

The 988 line (US) is a thoughtful inclusion — reviewers note these.

## What's deliberately NOT here

- A Sign-in section (no auth in v1)
- A Subscription / Refund section (no IAP in v1)
- A "User-Generated Content" moderation section (no public UGC in v1)
- An "Account Deletion" section in legal text (handled via the Reset App Data feature, described in Privacy Policy)

These can be added when we re-enable the corresponding features in v1.1+.
