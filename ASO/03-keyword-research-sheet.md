After launch, check Google Play Console > Acquire Users > Store Listing Visitors > Search Terms for:

1. Which search terms drive the most impressions

3. Which competitor brand terms send traffic your way

5. Which high-volume terms you're missing (add to description)

**Review cadence:** Weekly for first month, bi-weekly after.
