## Google Play ASO Rules (2026)

| Field | Limit | Indexing Weight | Notes |
|-------|-------|----------------|-------|
| App Title | **30 characters** | HIGHEST | Brand + 1-2 keywords |
| Short Description | **80 characters** | HIGH | Action-oriented, keyword-rich |
| Long Description | **4,000 characters** | MEDIUM | Keywords 3-5x naturally |
| Icon | 512x512 | CONVERSION | Must stand out at 48x48 |
| Feature Graphic | 1024x500 | CONVERSION | Shown in search results |
| Screenshots | Up to 8 | CONVERSION | First 3 matter most |
| Video | 30s-2min | HIGH (2026 trend) | Short-form preferred |

**2026 Rules:** No emojis/emoticons in title or short description. No performance claims ("#1", "best", "free"). Google Play increasingly surfaces apps via video-driven discovery and behavioral signals, not just keywords.

## Part 1: App Title Strategy

### Recommended Title Options (30 char max):

**Option A (Recommended):**
```
Celestia: AI Astrology Guide
```
- 29 characters
- Hits: AI (untapped), Astrology (high volume), Guide (warm/helpful intent)
- "Guide" mirrors CHANI's successful warm positioning
- "AI" differentiates from every competitor except Hint

**Option B:**
```
Celestia: Horoscope & Charts
```
- 30 characters
- Hits: Horoscope (highest volume), Charts (birth chart adjacent)
- More conventional, stronger keyword coverage
- Less differentiated

**Option C:**
```
Celestia: Daily Astrology AI
```
- 28 characters
- Hits: Daily (habit keyword), Astrology, AI
- Emphasizes daily usage and AI chat — both unique features
- "Daily" captures horoscope searchers indirectly

### Title Recommendation: **Go with Option A**

Rationale: "AI Astrology Guide" is a blue-ocean phrase nobody owns. It positions Celestia as both technologically advanced AND warm/helpful. It targets the growing "AI astrology" search trend while avoiding the cold "personalized" language of Co-Star/Nebula.

## Part 4: Icon Strategy

### Current Landscape:
- 80% of competitors use purple/dark cosmic themes
- Stars, moons, constellations are overused
- Co-Star stands out with minimalist black/white text icon

### Celestia Icon Recommendation:

**Concept:** Gold crescent moon or celestial symbol on deep navy (#0E0E22) background.

**Why this works:**
- Gold (#C8A84B) on navy is unique in the category — nobody uses warm gold
- Instantly differentiates from the purple/cosmic crowd at 48x48 thumbnail size
- Matches the in-app design system (consistency builds trust)
- Gold = premium, warm, personal — matches Celestia's positioning
- Simple enough to be recognizable at all sizes

**Avoid:**
- Purple/gradient backgrounds (blends into competitors)
- Complex illustrations (lost at small sizes)
- Text in the icon (doesn't scale)
- Stars/sparkles (generic)

## Part 8: Rating & Review Strategy

### Target: 4.7+ stars within first 90 days

**When to Prompt for Review:**
- After first "wow" moment (AI chat response that references specific placement)
- After completing birth chart exploration (3+ planets tapped)
- After Day 3 streak completion
- After sharing a screenshot or compatibility result
- NEVER on first session, NEVER during onboarding, NEVER after a paywall

**Review Prompt Copy:**
```
"Loving Celestia? A quick review helps other people
find personal astrology guidance too."
[Rate Now] [Maybe Later]
```

**Responding to Negative Reviews (Google Play Console):**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Acknowledge the specific issue
- Provide fix timeline or workaround
- Never be defensive — Mia reads review responses before downloading

### Review Keyword Seeding:
Users naturally mention features they love. To encourage keyword-rich reviews:
- After AI chat: "How was your astrology chat experience?"
- After compatibility: "Did the compatibility reading feel accurate?"
- After daily briefing: "Is your daily horoscope helpful?"
