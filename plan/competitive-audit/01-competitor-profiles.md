# Competitor Profiles

Detailed profiles of Co-Star, The Pattern, Sanctuary, Nebula, plus Celestia for reference. Compiled May 2026 from web research + code audit.

---

## 1. Co-Star

### Visual design
- **Palette:** Pure black background, white type, occasional grayscale gradients. The defining "anti-mystical" palette.
- **Typography:** Brutalist sans-serif with tight tracking, large display sizes, intentional negative space. Reads like art-zine.
- **Iconography:** Minimal — line glyphs, dots. No zodiac symbols in chrome.
- **Mode:** Dark only.

### Retention loop
- **Push 1×/day:** "Your day at a glance" — cryptic, sometimes mean copy. Examples: *"Your heart busts its knuckles against society"*, *"be slow and strategic like a mushroom"*, *"do your laundry. Fold it immediately."*
- **Notifications became memes** — viral on Twitter/Instagram for years. Screenshot-and-share is a built-in growth loop.
- **No streaks, no XP, no badges, no quests.**
- **Friends list with side-by-side compatibility** — only competitor that cracked social. Friend-add is hard-coded into onboarding, not optional.
- **AI chat:** "Ask the stars" — limited, paywalled.

### Subscription
- Free: daily push, basic chart, friend compatibility (limited).
- **Co-Star Plus** ~$14.99/mo or $99.99/yr — deeper interpretations, full natal chart of non-users, "Eros" relationship guidance, ask-the-stars Q&A, transit details.
- No standard free trial.

### Onboarding
- ~11 single-purpose screens.
- Birth date, exact time, birth city.
- **Aha moment:** instant brutalist natal chart + first cryptic line, then immediate friend-add prompt.

### MOAT
**The voice.** No competitor has matched the editorial tone. Plus the only working social graph in astrology.

### App Store complaints (themes)
- Recent paywall expansion ($14.99/mo from previously-free features) — long-time users feel betrayed
- Negative tone triggers anxiety
- "Why am I paying $9–15/mo when ChatGPT can do this?"
- Widget bugs

## 2. The Pattern

### Visual design
- **Palette:** Soft pastel lavenders/blues over dark-mode base; teal accents; gradient washes. Wellness-app coded.
- **Typography:** Modern humanist sans (likely Inter/SF). Long-form paragraph-first layout.
- **Iconography:** Almost zero astrology glyphs. Hides the astrology vocabulary deliberately — translates planetary mechanics into plain psychology language.
- **Mode:** Dark primary.

### Retention loop
- **Push:** "Go Deeper" into a current pattern or timing window. Earnest, therapeutic tone.
- **No streaks, no XP, no badges.**
- **Bonds tab** — relationship compatibility (friends, partners, family).
- **Connect dating sub-feature** — categorizes matches as soulmate / extraordinary / powerful / meaningful / complex / delicate / challenging.
- **No AI chat** — content is pre-generated long-form text + audio narratives.

### Subscription
- Free: core "Your Pattern" personality breakdown + limited timing.
- **Go Deeper+** $14.99/mo or $29.99/3mo — unlimited content, audio insights, sound meditations, life timeline.
- **Connect+** quarterly only — unlocks dating chat.
- Add-on profiles $9.99 each.
- Limited free preview as trial.

### Onboarding
- Name → gender → birth date → birth time → birth city.
- Allows skipping time but warns it degrades accuracy.
- **Aha moment:** instant render of "Your Pattern" — multi-paragraph psychological portrait that reads like a therapist's intake. Channing Tatum's 2019 viral reaction (now investor) still drives discovery.

### MOAT
**"Scarily accurate" psychological portrait + Bonds.** Translation of astrology into quotable plain-English personality language.

### App Store complaints (themes)
- Aggressive paywalling of previously-free features (1-star revenge reviews)
- Fatalistic tone — describes effects without prescriptive advice
- "Bonds" was free, now gated

## 3. Sanctuary

### Visual design
- **Palette:** Deep purples, midnight blues, occasional rose gold. Most "traditionally mystical" of the four.
- **Typography:** Modern sans body + decorative serif headings.
- **Iconography:** Zodiac glyphs, planetary symbols, tarot card art. Rich illustrated style.
- **Mode:** Dark primary.

### Retention loop
- **Daily horoscopes** by sign, plus weekly/monthly/annual.
- **Lessons tab** feeds astrology basics in chat-like conversation format.
- **No memorable push voice.**
- **No streaks, no XP, no badges.**
- **No peer-to-peer social.**
- **AI chat:** Limited; the differentiator is *human* chat, not AI.

### Subscription
- Free: daily horoscope, basic chart.
- **Sanctuary+ membership** ~$19.99/mo — Explore content library, expanded horoscopes (yesterday/today/tomorrow + week), tarot, crystal, compatibility calculator, monthly 5-min reading credit.
- **Live readings (à la carte):** $2.99/min in 10/15/20/30-min blocks. Intro $4.99 first 5 minutes.

### Onboarding
- Birth date → birth time → birth city → sign-up.
- Some user-reported bugs in city field.
- **Aha moment is weak** — the actual hook lands when user starts a $4.99 live chat with a real astrologer.

### MOAT
**Real human astrologers/psychics on demand via text chat.** Talkspace for astrology — the differentiator no automated app can replicate.

### App Store complaints (themes)
- Birth chart accuracy bugs (placements changing after re-install)
- $20/mo membership only includes one 15-min reading
- Generic readings from random astrologers
- Per-minute pricing escalates fast

## 4. Nebula

### Visual design
- **Palette:** Cosmic gradients — deep blues, violets, magenta highlights, starfield backgrounds.
- **Typography:** Modern geometric sans, large numerical readouts for compatibility scores and energy percentages.
- **Iconography:** Planetary glyphs, tarot illustrations, animated zodiac wheels.
- **Signature:** **Energy meters** — physical/emotional/intellectual percentage rings, borrowed from biorhythm/wellness app conventions.

### Retention loop
- Daily horoscope + Card of the Day tarot pull + 3-metric energy tracker.
- Standard daily horoscope notifications, not memetic.
- **No streaks/XP/badges prominent.** Some daily ritual prompts.
- AI compatibility insights (not flagship).
- **1,000+ vetted spiritual advisors** for paid 1:1 readings — same model as Sanctuary, larger inventory.

### Subscription
- Weekly $7.99 (with 3-day free trial), monthly ~$24.99, 3-month $29.99–$39.99, annual $49.99 (varies by cohort/test).
- **Live psychic readings:** First 3 min free, then ~$3.99/min.

### Onboarding
- Birth date → time → place → relationship status → goals/concerns selection (used for paywall messaging).
- **Aha moment:** energy meters lighting up + first psychic-advisor browse with photos and ratings. Feels closer to a marketplace than an oracle.

### MOAT
**Hybrid:** automated daily content + marketplace of human psychics. Lower-friction onboarding to a paid live reading than Sanctuary, with broader advisor inventory.

### App Store complaints (themes)
- Aggressive paywall after onboarding
- Weekly subscription dark-pattern ($7.99/wk = ~$415/yr)
- Inconsistent advisor quality
- Auto-renew complaints

## 5. Celestia (for reference)

### Visual design
- **Palette:** Navy `#0E0E22`, gold `#C8A84B`, cream `#FAF8F2`, warm `#F3EDE2`, stone `#97907F`, ink `#2A2418`.
- **Typography:** Playfair Display (serif headings), DM Sans (body).
- **Iconography:** Subtle line glyphs, planet symbols (☉ ☽ ↑ ♀ ♂ etc) used sparingly. No mystical illustrations.
- **Mode:** Light/dark via ThemeContext.
- **Hero gradients:** warm muted plum-charcoal tones (not electric blue/purple). Border radius 24 on hero corners.
- **Signature:** Editorial-luxury aesthetic — closer to Aesop / Cereal magazine / Notion than to other astrology apps.

### Retention loop
- **Daily briefing** with time-of-day-aware hero, AI-generated navigator headline + summary, navigate-toward / navigate-around (do/avoid with reasons), 5 life-area cards, journal prompt, quests, energy scores, Mercury Rx card, lunar event card, share cards.
- **5-tier XP system** (Day One → Curious → Engaged → Active → Anchored) with streak multipliers + first-action-of-day 2× bonus.
- **20 badges** + 5 hidden surprise badges + D7/D14/D30 surprise rolls (30/40/55%).
- **Streak system** with 1 free freeze + freeze replenish every 7 days + restore-with-freeze offer.
- **Push notifications:** 7 channels — cosmic_morning (anchored to user's wake time), evening_reflection, transit_alerts, streak_guardian, reactivation, cosmic_milestones, weekly_digest.
- **Lapse cascade** D2/D3/D5/D7/D10/D14/D21 with personalized copy (partner / chat / chart tier).
- **Anticipation pushes** at D6/D13/D27 (one-day-before-milestone).
- **Badge rescue push** when user is 1-2 actions away from unlocking.
- **Event-based pushes**: partner-added → +2d insight; chat sent → +24h follow-up (cancel-on-return); journal pattern → Friday push.
- **Surprise insight system** at D4/D10/D17/D24 with 30% roll, dedup'd.
- **D7 first-week recap** card (Gemini-generated, share-ready).
- **D28 Moon Cycle pattern** card (Gemini-generated).
- **Briefing-mode rotation** weeks 1-4: Standard → Pattern → Partner → Archetype.
- **Internal-trigger morning templates** activate after week 2.
- **Indecision-keyword journal mining** → tailored chat suggestion chip.
- **Pro-only daily insight** card (Gemini, extra layer for paid users).
- **Pro week-1 recap** at Day 7 of being Pro (Gemini, two flavors).
- **NPS sentiment prompt** every 30 days, feeds health score.
- **At-risk intervention banner** when health_score < 40.
- **Streak restore offer** modal on broken 7+ day streak.
- **Solar-return push** 7 days before user's birthday.
- **Trial-end push** 2 days before trial charge.
- **Pro feature-discovery push** Day 3 + Day 7 of Pro for highest-rank untried feature.

### AI chat
- Gemini-powered (3-model fallback chain).
- 10 free messages/day. Pro = unlimited.
- 16 initial suggestion questions + 6 follow-ups per topic + time-of-day variants.
- Chat sessions saved, partner context can be injected.
- V1 language override: psychological-frame-first output (App Store compliance).

### Compatibility (Circle)
- 8 relationship types: Partner / Friend / Parent / Sibling / Boss / Colleague / Child / Other.
- Zodiac-only mode (no birth time/city needed).
- Full synastry score + AI core / details / insights.
- 3 partners free; Pro = unlimited.

### Subscription
- $6.99/mo or $49.99/yr.
- 7-day free trial.
- Welcome to Pro screen (post-purchase) with 3 hero cards.
- Cancel flow with 8 reasons (or 7 trial-specific reasons) + REASON × VARIANT save offers + iOS Settings deep-link.

### Onboarding
- 14 steps including: hook, motivation, pain point, depth selection, birth date, birth time, birth place, calculating, first hit, big reveal (chart wheel + Big 3 + 2 deeply specific reveal statements from MOON_HOUSE_INSIGHTS / SUN_MOON_COMBOS / VENUS_SIGN_INSIGHTS), daily hook (with wake-time picker), soft paywall, reassurance, hard close.
- Birth-time-unknown path exists ("I'm not sure → noon chart").
- Wake-time question drives morning-push schedule.
- **Aha moment:** chart reveal + Big 3 + 2 chart-derived deeply specific statements (e.g., "You only fully feel yourself when reflected in someone else's eyes" for 7H moon).

### MOAT
**The most retention-engineered app in the category.** No competitor has streaks/XP/badges/AI-chat-as-flagship/event-based-pushes/surprise-insight-system/briefing-mode-rotation. The engagement scaffolding is genuinely unique.

### Visual / brand position
**Editorial-luxury, navigator framing.** Sits in unclaimed visual + tonal territory between Co-Star (mean/brutalist) and The Pattern (fatalistic/pastel). Closer to Aesop / Cereal magazine than to other astrology apps.

### Where Celestia is weakest (the audit's actual finding)
1. **No social graph.** Co-Star's friends list and The Pattern's Bonds drive significant retention. Celestia's Circle is private (you add other people's charts, but they don't see you back). 1-star vs Co-Star here.
2. **No memorable push voice.** Co-Star's notifications are quoted in articles years later. Celestia's are personalized but not screenshot-able.
3. **No daily share-card moment with viral potential.** The competitors that share well do so because the content itself is the share asset. Celestia has share cards but none have gone viral.
