Input Scenarios — What Mia Actually Knows

Mia doesn't always have full birth data. Sometimes she only knows his sign from his Instagram bio. Sometimes she knows his exact birthday from his driver's license she glimpsed. The app must handle ALL of these gracefully — never blocking her, always giving value, and always showing her what MORE data would unlock.

Scenario A — Sign Only
"He's a Scorpio — that's all I know"
She saw his zodiac emoji in his Instagram bio. Or he mentioned it at the bar. She has zero other data.
Input: Just his Sun sign
Output: Sun sign × Sun sign compatibility — general dynamic, attraction pattern, tension points. 1-2 page preview.
Accuracy level: ~30% — like reading the chapter titles without the book.
Upgrade nudge: "This is Virgo + Scorpio in general. Want to see what YOUR specific charts say? Add his birthday for a deeper reading."
FREE tier. This is the hook. She gets enough to be curious, not enough to be satisfied.
Scenario B — Birthday Only
"His birthday is November 7, 1997"
She knows his birthday from Instagram, text exchange, or she asked. No birth time, no city.
Input: Full birthday (no time, no city)
What we can calculate: Sun, Moon sign (approximate — Moon changes sign every 2.5 days, so we note the ambiguity), Mercury, Venus, Mars positions.
Output: Medium-depth compatibility — Sun interaction, Venus-Venus dynamic, Mars chemistry. Much richer than sign-only.
Upgrade nudge: "We can see your Sun-Venus-Mars dynamics, but his Moon sign is uncertain today. If you can find his birth time, we'll unlock the emotional and communication layers."
FREE preview + Premium for full analysis. This is the most common scenario — 70% of checks will be birthday-only.
Scenario C — Full Birth Data
"November 7, 1997, 3:42am, Dallas TX"
She asked. Or she found out from his mom. Or he's a Celestia user and shared his chart link. This is the gold scenario.
Input: Full birthday + birth time + birth city
What we can calculate: Everything. Complete natal chart for both. Synastry aspects. House overlays. Exact Moon, Rising, all planets.
Output: Full compatibility deep-dive. 14-18 page report. Every section unlocked. This is the $9.99 report.
Accuracy: Maximum. This is real synastry analysis.
PREMIUM — Full report. The holy grail. This is what she pays $9.99 for and screenshots every page.
Scenario D — He's Already on Celestia
"He sent me his Celestia chart link"
Best case: he's already a user. She taps his shared link, they're auto-connected. Both charts are complete.
Input: One-tap connection via shared link
Output: Instant full compatibility — no data entry needed
Bonus: Both users see the report. Both get notified of transit impacts on their relationship. Ongoing dynamic — not a one-time report.
PREMIUM — Ongoing relationship insights. "This week, Mercury retrograde is hitting BOTH your communication houses. Be extra patient with each other."
The Progressive Disclosure Principle

Every scenario gives value. No scenario hits a dead end. The less data she has, the more Celestia says "here's what we CAN see — and here's what you'd unlock with more." She's always moving forward. She never feels punished for not having his birth time. She feels rewarded for adding more data.

Input UX Rules
Name is optional — but if she enters "Jake," the report says "You and Jake" everywhere. Personalization = emotional investment = higher conversion.

Birthday = minimum for real value — but sign-only still works. Never block her.

Birth time = "if you know it" — with a playful hint: "Ask him! Or check his birth certificate next time you're at his place 😏"

Birth city — only matters with birth time. Hide this field until time is entered.
Sign-only tap grid — for when she literally only knows "he's a Scorpio." One tap and she's in. Zero friction.

Relationship type selector — After entering their info, quick toggle: "This is my..." → Crush / Partner / Ex / Friend / Family. Changes the tone of the report.

Remember past checks — She checked Jake last week. When she opens compatibility again, Jake's card is there. One tap to re-read. No re-entering data.

Add more data later — She checked Jake with sign-only. She just found out his birthday. She updates — the report deepens instantly. Reward, not restart.

Relationship Types — Not Just Romance

Compatibility isn't only about dating. Mia also checks her best friend, her mom, her coworker. Each relationship type adjusts the tone, sections, and language of the report.

💕
Crush / Partner
The default. Includes chemistry, physical attraction, love languages, long-term potential. Written with romantic energy. "Where you'll fight" section is about relationship patterns. The #1 use case — 65% of all checks.
Purchased 3-4x/year per Mia
💔
Ex
Same structure but different tone. Less "will this work" and more "here's WHY it didn't work" and "here's what you were actually attracted to." Helps her process and move on. Validation for leaving. Clarity on the pattern.
Highest emotional intensity. Usually purchased late at night.
👯‍♀️
Best Friend
No romance sections. Focus on communication styles, emotional needs, where you clash, why you're loyal to each other. "Why you fight" becomes playful. Designed to be read TOGETHER — shared during brunch, screenshotted for Stories.
Most shareable. "Tag your bestie" content engine.
👩‍👧
Family (Mom, Sister)
Deeper, more emotional. "Why your mom drives you crazy" through astrology. Moon-Moon dynamics are the focus. Helps her understand generational patterns. Gentler tone. "Your mom isn't trying to control you — her Cancer Moon just processes love as worry."
Surprisingly emotional. Often leads to deeper conversations.
💼
Coworker / Boss
Work dynamics. Communication friction. Why her boss micromanages (Saturn placement). Why her coworker takes credit (Leo Mars). Practical, useful, lighter tone. Not available at launch — Month 4-5 add.
Future addition. Expands use case beyond personal life.
🤳
Celebrity
"Am I compatible with Timothée Chalamet?" — fun, sharable, drives TikTok content. Pre-built charts for popular celebrities. She checks, screenshots, posts "apparently me and Harry Styles are a 92% match 🤷‍♀️". Pure viral fuel.
Free feature. Purely viral — drives shares + downloads.

Non-Negotiable Rules for Compatibility
Always
✓ Give value at every data level — sign-only still gets a reading
✓ Show what more data unlocks — "add his birthday for deeper insights"
✓ Use their names if entered — "You and Jake" not "Person 1 and Person 2"
✓ The first section is FREE and amazing — the hook must land before the paywall
✓ Blurred sections are visible — she sees the titles, reads 1 blurred line, knows what she's missing
✓ Share link on every result — one-tap send to the other person
✓ Remember past checks — Jake's card stays. She can re-read anytime.
✓ Honest, never doom-y — even bad compatibility gets "here's the challenge + here's how to navigate it"
Never
✗ Block her with "need more data" — always give something, even with just a sign
✗ Say "you're not compatible" — no binary yes/no. Astrology is about dynamics, not verdicts
✗ Use a simple number score alone — 78% means nothing without the story behind it
✗ Make the free preview garbage — if the free section isn't specific enough to shock her, she won't pay
✗ Generic sign-level filler — "Virgo and Scorpio are both determined" is not an insight
✗ Hard paywall before ANY value — she must see at least one "how does it know" moment for free
✗ Forget the share mechanic — if the share button isn't prominent, we lose the viral loop
✗ Scare her — "this relationship is doomed" is never acceptable, even if the charts clash badly

The Compatibility Quality Test

"If she reads the free preview and doesn't feel an URGENT need to read the blurred sections — the preview isn't good enough. If she reads the full report and doesn't send it to the other person — the report isn't personal enough. If the other person doesn't enter their own data after receiving the link — the share flow isn't smooth enough."
