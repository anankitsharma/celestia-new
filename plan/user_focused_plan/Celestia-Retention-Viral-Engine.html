The Messaging Architecture — Push + Email Working Together

Daily habit builder. The morning note. The transit alert. The "come back" nudge. Push is how she sees us every morning without opening the app. It's the alarm clock for the habit loop. Short, warm, lands on her lock screen.

Revenue driver + re-engagement. The weekly transit (Monday 8am, Premium conversion trigger). The birthday Solar Return (8-12% report conversion). The win-back sequence for lapsed users. Longer, richer, and drives the biggest purchases.

Both push + email: Mercury Retrograde start (push = alert, email = full survival guide), Eclipse season (push = heads up, email = what it means for her chart), new report available, annual subscription offer

05
Win-Back Flows — When She Goes Quiet
Re-Engagement Sequence — Gentle, Never Desperate
🔔
Day 3 of inactivity — Push only
(No special message. Daily insight continues as normal.)
The daily insight is itself the re-engagement. She sees it on her lock screen even if she doesn't open the app. The insight should be ESPECIALLY compelling on days 3-5 — tied to a real transit. "Something is shifting in your love life this week. Tap to see what." Don't say "we miss you." Just be useful.
🔔
Day 5-7 — Contextual push
"The full moon hits your 7th house tomorrow. You might want to see this."
Tied to a REAL astrological event that's actually happening. Not fake urgency. Not "you haven't opened the app in 5 days." She comes back because something real is happening in her chart, not because we guilted her.
📧
Day 10 — Email: "A Lot Has Changed in Your Chart"
Email with specific transit updates she missed
"While you were away, Mars entered your career house and Venus moved into your 8th house. Here's what that means for the next 2 weeks." Real content. Real value. She opens it, reads something that resonates, and taps "Open in App →".
📧
Day 21 — Last email: "Your Chart Misses You (kidding. but also not.)"
Warm, playful, one last try
Light tone. "Your chart is still here. We haven't peeked at it without you (promise). But there's a LOT happening in your 7th house right now, and we think you'd want to know." If she doesn't return after this, stop emailing. No further messages. Respect the silence. If she ever re-opens the app, the daily insight resumes and Celestia says "Welcome back, Mia ✦ A lot has happened in your chart. Let me catch you up."
🫣
What respectful re-engagement feels like to Mia
"I didn't open the app for like 10 days because I was busy. I didn't get a single 'we miss you!!' notification. I just kept seeing the daily insight on my lock screen. Then I got an email that said Mars entered my career house and honestly that's exactly what I needed to hear because I've been stressed about work. I opened the app and it said 'welcome back — a lot has happened.' It felt like a friend who respected my space but was there when I came back."
Never desperate. Never guilty. Just useful, warm, and respectful. That's how you win someone back.

The Viral Mechanics — 5 Sharing Triggers Built Into the Product

Celestia doesn't have a referral program. It doesn't need one. The product IS the viral mechanism. Every core feature has a sharing trigger built in. She doesn't share because we ask — she shares because the content is too personal and too accurate NOT to send to someone.

💕
Trigger 1: Compatibility Check = 2 Users
Every compatibility check involves TWO people. She enters Jake's birthday. She gets the preview. Below: "Send to Jake — he can add his birth details for a deeper reading." Jake receives a link. He enters his data. He's now a user. Cost per acquisition: $0. Every check = at minimum 1 new user acquired.
Estimated viral coefficient: 0.7 new users per check
📸
Trigger 2: Screenshot → Stories = 3-8 Viewers
She screenshots the daily insight, a planet card, a compatibility score, or a chat response. She posts it to IG Stories with "this app is scary accurate 😭." Her 800 followers see it. 12 tap through. 4 visit the site. 1-2 download. Every screenshot = a mini billboard. Celestia ✦ branding visible on everything.
Estimated viral coefficient: 0.3 new users per screenshot shared
🔗
Trigger 3: Chart Link = Personal Landing Page
celestia.com/chart/mia — a beautiful web page showing her Big 3 summary. She shares this link in her social bios, group chats, or dating profiles. Every person who clicks sees her chart AND a "Get yours free →" CTA at the bottom. Her chart link is a permanent funnel.
Estimated viral coefficient: 0.15 new users per link shared
💬
Trigger 4: Chat Screenshot = "What App Is This??"
She screenshots a chat response where the AI says something scarily accurate. She sends it to her group chat: "look what this app told me about why I self-sabotage 😭." 3 friends reply "WHAT APP." The AI's specificity is the viral mechanic. Generic answers = no screenshots. Scary-accurate answers = instant sharing.
Estimated viral coefficient: 0.5 new users per chat screenshot shared
📲
Trigger 5: Compatibility Report Share = Revenue + New User
She buys the $9.99 compatibility report. At the bottom: "Share this report with Jake — he'll see the same insights from his perspective." Jake clicks → enters the app → sees the report → gets hooked. She paid AND brought in a new user. Revenue event + acquisition event in one action.

✓ Daily push at 7:15-7:30am — never skip, never send 2
✓ Tie every push to her ACTUAL chart — never generic
✓ Re-engagement uses real transits, not guilt
✓ Email has value IN the email — not "click to find out"
✓ Birthday email at 8am on the EXACT day
✓ Stop emailing after Day 21 of inactivity — respect her silence
✓ "Welcome back" message when she re-opens after absence
✓ Weekly email on Monday 8am — the Premium conversion engine
✓ Max 2 emails per week (weekly + 1 event). Never more.
Retention — Never
✗ "We miss you!" — desperate and generic
✗ Push notifications after 10pm — respect her sleep
✗ More than 1 push per day (except transit alerts on event days)
✗ Email with no value — every email must be worth reading even without clicking
✗ Fake urgency ("your chart is expiring!") — she'll lose trust permanently
✗ Emailing lapsed users forever — 3 attempts then stop
✗ Selling in every email — 80% value, 20% conversion
✗ Generic "all Virgos" push — it's HER chart or nothing
Viral — Always
✓ Celestia ✦ branding on everything screenshottable
✓ Share buttons on every result (compatibility, chart, daily, chat)
✓ Compatibility link sends the OTHER person into the funnel
✓ Chart links are beautiful standalone web pages with CTA
✓ "Share to Stories" generates a pre-designed card, not a raw screenshot
✓ Design every feature asking "will she screenshot this?"
✓ The AI's responses must be specific enough to be share-worthy
Viral — Never
✗ "Invite 3 friends for a free month!" — forced referral programs feel desperate
✗ Pop-ups asking her to share — she'll share when the content earns it
✗ Generic content that nobody would screenshot
