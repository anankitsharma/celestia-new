01
Product Vision

Celestia is the warm, honest friend who happens to know astrology deeply — the one Mia texts at 10:30pm when she can't figure out why she keeps ending up in the same situation.

Not a horoscope app. Not a chart calculator. Not a cold data tool. Celestia is a mirror — it shows Mia her own patterns in language she already uses, at a price she can afford, in the exact moments she needs it most.

👤
Why this vision exists
"I don't need another app that tells me I'm a Virgo. I need something that explains why I keep choosing the same type of guy and what I'm supposed to do about it — without charging me $200 like a therapist."
This is the gap in the market. No one is filling it with warmth.
Brand Personality Warm, honest, slightly irreverent best friend — not a mystical guru. Because Mia is turned off by "cosmic energy" language. She wants someone who talks like her smart friend.
Visual Identity Desert Dawn palette — terracotta, warm sand, sage. Light mode. Because Mia shops at Free People, saves boho Pinterest boards, and every competitor is dark mode. We own warmth.
Core Promise "Your chart explains the pattern." Not predictions. Not destiny. Patterns. Because Mia doesn't want fortune-telling. She wants to understand WHY things keep happening.
02
Core Features — Built for Mia

Every feature exists because Mia has a specific need at a specific moment. Nothing is built because "competitors have it."

🤖
AI Chat — "Ask Celestia"
She types her real question in plain language. No astrology jargon needed. The AI answers using her specific chart placements — not generic sun sign advice.
Because Mia doesn't type "explain my Venus-Neptune square." She types "why do I keep attracting unavailable men." The AI must understand both and answer in her language.
📊
Birth Chart — Visual & Personal
Beautiful, shareable visualization of her natal chart. Each placement has a warm, 2-sentence explanation she actually understands. Not a textbook — a mirror.
Because Mia screenshots pretty things. If the chart is ugly, she won't share it. If the explanations use jargon, she won't read them. Beauty + clarity = screenshots = viral loop.
💕
Compatibility Checker
Enter anyone's birth data — crush, ex, best friend, mom. Get a detailed analysis of the dynamic: strengths, tension points, and what to watch for.
Because the FIRST thing Mia does when she meets a new guy is ask "what's your birthday?" This feature is the #1 reason she'll send someone a link. Every check = 2 users. Growth disguised as a feature.
🌅
Daily Personalized Insight
Every morning: a push notification based on today's transits hitting HER chart. Not "Virgos will feel emotional." Instead: "Venus trines your natal Moon today — conversations about feelings will flow easier than usual."
Because this is what makes Mia open the app EVERY DAY. Co-Star's daily notification is the #1 retention mechanic in the category. But theirs are vague. Ours are specific to her chart + actionable. She'll screenshot them.
📅
Weekly Transit Report
Delivered every Monday 8am. A personalized briefing: what's happening in her chart this week, what to expect, what to watch for. Like a weather forecast for her emotional life.
Because Monday morning is when Mia feels the most anxious about the week ahead. This gives her a sense of "I know what's coming" — which is exactly what astrology gives people. Control in a chaotic world. Premium feature — the weekly report is what converts free users.
📄
Downloadable Reports
Beautiful, permanent PDFs: Full birth chart report, compatibility deep-dive, year-ahead forecast. Something she keeps, re-reads, highlights, sends to friends.
Because the chat disappears. The daily notification is fleeting. A report is a PRODUCT she owns. It feels like buying a book about herself. $9.99 per report for non-subscribers, free for Premium members. This is the one-time purchase path for people who won't subscribe.

Viral Loop — Mia IS the Marketing
4 Built-In Viral Mechanics

📸 Screenshot-Worthy Design

Every chart, every AI answer, every daily insight is designed to be beautiful enough to screenshot. If it's ugly, she won't share it. The visual quality of the output IS the marketing. Desert Dawn palette. Clean typography. Instagram-ready without editing.

🔗 Shareable Chart Links

One-tap "Share your chart" generates: celestia.com/chart/mia — a beautiful web page showing her chart summary. At the bottom: "Want to see YOUR chart?" Every shared link = a landing page = a new user. The product IS the distribution.

💕 Compatibility = 2 Users

"Check our compatibility!" — she sends the link to her date. He enters HIS birth data to see the result. Now he's a user too. Every compatibility check = 2 users acquired. Growth disguised as a feature. Costs $0.

💬 Group Chat Contagion

Mia sends an AI answer to her 4-person group chat. 3 of 4 friends enter their birth data within 24 hours. Each of them sends to THEIR group chats. If viral coefficient >1.0, growth is self-sustaining with $0 ad spend.

💬
In Mia's Group Chat
"okay I know you're going to think I'm crazy but this app just described my entire relationship history and I'm literally shaking. just put in your birthday. trust me."
3 of 4 friends sign up that night. One of them upgrades to Premium within a week.
