Instagram's Role — Different From TikTok

TikTok = DISCOVERY

She finds us. She watches. She laughs. She maybe taps profile. The content reaches STRANGERS. It's a megaphone. Optimized for reach and views from non-followers. 55% of Reel views come from non-followers.

Instagram = ENGAGEMENT + IDENTITY

She follows us. She saves carousels. She shares Reels to her Story. She DMs posts to her group chat. She screenshots her chart and posts it. Instagram isn't about discovery — it's about deepening the relationship and making Celestia part of her social identity.
The 2026 Instagram Algorithm — What Matters

DM Shares

The #1 signal for reaching new audiences. 694K Reels are sent via DM every minute. Content worth sharing = growth.

Saves

Carousels are the saves king. A save = "I want to come back to this." Carousels with 10.15% avg engagement dominate saves.

Watch Time

For Reels: watch-through rate is everything. A 15sec Reel fully watched beats a 60sec Reel abandoned at 30%. Keep it tight.

03
Grid Aesthetic — The Desert Dawn Feed
Visual Identity — Standing Out in Her Feed
The Desert Dawn palette:
→ Primary: terracotta (#C17F59), warm cream (#F5EDE3)
→ Secondary: sage green, dusty lavender, muted gold
→ Backgrounds: deep navy/charcoal (#0F0D1A) for dark posts, warm cream for light
→ Typography: Playfair Display (headings), DM Sans (body)

Why this stands out: Most astrology accounts use either neon purple/blue (Co-Star aesthetic) or pastel pink (generic spiritual). Celestia's warm, earthy tones are UNIQUE on her feed. She scrolls and sees blue, blue, bright, bright, then — warm terracotta. Her eye stops.
Grid pattern (every 3 posts):
→ Post 1: Carousel (dark bg, terracotta accents)
→ Post 2: Reel (thumbnail designed with text overlay)
→ Post 3: Carousel or single image (cream/light bg)

This creates a visual rhythm: dark-video-light-dark-video-light. The grid looks intentional without being rigid. When she taps the profile, the grid feels cohesive and beautiful — like a brand, not a random meme account.

Cover images for Reels: Always add a custom thumbnail. Never let Instagram auto-select a blurry frame. The thumbnail has text overlay in Celestia fonts on a solid color bg.

Carousels — The Engagement King (10% Avg Engagement)

Carousels are Celestia's secret weapon on Instagram. They drive the most saves, the most shares, and the highest engagement rate of any format. Mia doesn't just scroll them — she saves them, screenshots individual slides, and sends them to friends. Every carousel is designed to be swiped, saved, and shared.

Carousel Design Rules
Format: 1080×1350px (4:5) — fills the feed, more real estate than square.
Slides: 7-10 per carousel. Instagram allows up to 20 but sweet spot is 7-10 for astrology content.
Slide 1: Must stop the scroll. Bold headline + sign symbol + "swipe →" hint. No body text.
Middle slides: One idea per slide. Large text. Plenty of whitespace. She should be able to screenshot ANY single slide and it makes sense standalone.
Last slide: Always has a CTA — either "tag someone," "comment your sign," or "link in bio." This drives engagement on the final frame.
Typography: Playfair Display for headlines, DM Sans for body. Max 40 words per slide.
Brand mark: Small "Celestia ✦" watermark on every slide — visible but not obnoxious. Every screenshot = a tiny billboard.
05
Reels — The Reach Engine (55% Non-Follower Views)
Reels Strategy — Cross-Post + Native
70% cross-posted from TikTok (re-edited)
The same scripts work — but we MUST re-edit for Instagram. No TikTok watermark (Instagram penalizes this). No CapCut watermark. Re-export clean from the editing app. Same content, fresh export.

30% Instagram-native Reels
Content designed specifically for Instagram's culture: slightly more polished, slightly more aesthetic, and optimized for DM sharing (the #1 ranking signal on IG in 2026).

Reel length: 15-30 seconds for most. Never over 45 seconds unless the hook is incredible. Instagram rewards completion rate — shorter = higher completion.
Instagram-native Reel ideas:
→ "Send this to the Scorpio in your life" — designed for DM sharing
→ "Save this for when he leaves you on read" — designed for saves
→ Screen recording of someone exploring their Celestia chart — beautiful app footage
→ "What your rising sign says about your first impression" — talking head, warm aesthetic

Custom thumbnails: Always. Text overlay on a solid Desert Dawn background. The grid should look cohesive when she visits the profile. Auto-selected frames look messy.

Trending audio: Add trending sounds to boost discovery. Instagram still pushes content using trending audio into Explore and Reels feeds.

Countdown: "Mercury retrograde starts..." → followers get notified when it arrives. Event-driven engagement.
Link sticker: On EVERY Story sequence — "Get your chart →" pointing to hicelestia.com/ig. Subtle but always present.

Non-Negotiable Rules
Always
✓ Carousels 3x/week minimum — they drive saves, the #1 engagement signal
✓ Reels 3-4x/week — they drive discovery from non-followers
✓ Stories DAILY — even just the transit energy + a poll
✓ Custom Reel thumbnails — keep the grid cohesive
✓ Celestia ✦ watermark on every carousel slide
✓ Desert Dawn palette consistently — warm tones, clean type
✓ Every post has a CTA — save, share, tag, link in bio, or comment
✓ Keywords in captions — IG SEO > hashtags in 2026
✓ Design every slide to be screenshot-worthy standalone
✓ Remove TikTok watermarks before cross-posting Reels
Never
✗ Post Reels with TikTok watermark — Instagram deprioritizes them
✗ Skip Stories for more than 1 day — breaks the daily connection
✗ Use more than 5 hashtags — hashtag stuffing hurts in 2026. Use keywords instead.
✗ Post carousels with walls of text — one idea per slide, max 40 words
✗ Let Instagram auto-select Reel thumbnails — always custom
✗ Post generic zodiac memes — Celestia's content is warm and specific, not recycled meme templates
✗ Ignore DMs — DM engagement drives the algorithm. Reply to every message.
✗ Break the visual grid pattern — consistency builds brand recognition at the profile level
