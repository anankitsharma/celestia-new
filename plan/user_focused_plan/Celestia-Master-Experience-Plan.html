The Core Principle — She Never "Navigates"
❌ What a "Features App" Feels Like

She opens the app. Sees 5 tabs at the bottom: Home, Chart, Chat, Compatibility, Profile. She thinks "where should I go?" She taps Chart. Reads something. Wants to ask a question about it. Goes back. Taps Chat. Re-explains what she was looking at. Gets an answer. Wants to check compatibility. Goes back. Taps Compatibility. Re-enters data. Every feature is an island. She's the ferry between them. It feels like using a tool. She stops coming back after a week.

✅ What an "Experience" Feels Like

She opens the app. Today's insight is there. She reads it. Below it: "Ask Celestia about this →" — she taps, the chat opens with today's transit already loaded. She asks about the guy. Celestia mentions her Venus. "Want to see your Venus placement? →" — she taps, her chart scrolls to Venus. She reads it. "Check compatibility with him? →" — she taps, enters his birthday, sees the result. "Get the full deep-dive →" — $9.99. She never left one continuous flow. She never "navigated." She just followed her curiosity.

The rule: every screen has at least one bridge to another screen. She never hits a dead end. She never has to think "what do I do now?" The next step is always visible, always relevant, always one tap away.

The Emotion Engine — The App Reads the Room

Celestia adapts its entire experience — home screen, prompts, chat tone, suggested features — based on signals about Mia's emotional state. She doesn't select a mood. The app infers it from her behavior.

Emotional State Detection — What the App Reads
Time signals:
→ 7am opening = morning routine (light, optimistic prompts)
→ 11pm opening = can't sleep (deeper, reflective prompts)
→ 2am opening = emotional crisis (gentlest tone, comfort mode)
→ Weekday lunch = quick break (snappy, practical)
→ Sunday evening = weekly anxiety (reassurance mode)
Behavior signals:
→ Opens chat immediately = she has something specific on her mind
→ Checks compatibility = new person or relationship anxiety
→ Re-reads old compatibility = thinking about someone from the past
→ Explores chart for 20+ min = curiosity/self-discovery mode
→ Opens app 3x in one day = something is happening in her life
😟
Anxious / Vulnerable
Detected: late night, sad language in chat, re-reading old reports
Home screen shifts to comfort mode. Prompts become softer: "How are you feeling tonight?" instead of "Check your compatibility!" Chat tone goes gentler — validation first, chart references second. Daily insight card shows a comforting message if she hasn't read it yet.
