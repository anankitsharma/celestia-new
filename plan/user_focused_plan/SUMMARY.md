## 10 Key Themes Across All Files

1. **The Mia Obsession** — Every single file returns to one specific person. All decisions flow from her reality, not market research.

2. **Bottom-Up Framework** — Don't start with "$12.8B astrology market." Start with one person's pain and build what she can't live without.

3. **Bridge Architecture** — No "features" — continuous flow: notification -> daily -> chat -> chart -> compatibility -> reports -> share. She never navigates; she follows curiosity.

4. **Peak Curiosity Monetization** — Paywall appears at the exact moment she WANTS more (blurred compatibility sections, weekly report preview, chart teaser), not when she's calm.

5. **Viral + Retention Synthesis**
   - Retention: Daily insights (365x), weekly anchor, memory layer, progress identity
   - Viral: Screenshots, two-person mechanics, group chat sharing, zero-CAC growth

6. **Emotion-First Design** — App detects her emotional state (anxious/excited/curious) and adapts home screen, tone, and upsells.

7. **Channel Discipline** — Each channel has one job:
   - TikTok = awareness (strangers discover)
   - Google SEO = conversion (high-intent searches)
   - Instagram = identity (saves, shares, Stories)
   - Email/Push = retention (weekly, daily)

8. **Memory as Moat** — After 6 months: 50+ chats, 5 compatibility checks, 12 reports. Switching = starting from zero.

9. **Design System** — Desert Dawn palette (terracotta, cream, sage, lavender). Warm, earthy, unique from neon competitors. Playfair Display + DM Sans. Friend voice, not guru.

10. **The Math** — 100 users who love you beat 10,000 who tolerate you. 1 Mia -> 3-5 friends -> each becomes a new Mia -> exponential.
