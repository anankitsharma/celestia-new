1. Co-Star — Negative Reviews & Complaints
Co-Star is the market leader with the most brand recognition, but has accumulated severe user backlash. Here are the most repeated complaints:

CO-STAR
Paywalled Core
Most basic features now locked behind a $9/month subscription. Long-time free users feel cheated overnight.
Deliberately Negative Tone
The app intentionally 'trolls' users with harsh, anxiety-inducing notifications. Founder admitted to this on a podcast. Multiple users deleted app because of messages like 'You talk about other people because you don't have your own life.'
No Real Astrologers
Co-Star has no professional astrologers on its team. Uses a non-standard house system (Porphyry) that most professional astrologers reject.
AI-Generated & Repetitive
Daily readings are clearly template-driven. Users notice the same structure day after day with minor word swaps. Content feels generic, not personalized.
Unexplained Terminology
Terms like 'Earth Venus' used with no explanation. Users cannot understand readings without already knowing astrology deeply.
Privacy Concerns
Requests phone number or social media login upfront. Some users felt readings were eerily personal — 'like it has access to my texts'.
No Actionable Guidance
Provides observations but no constructive advice. Tells you what is happening cosmically but not what to do about it.
Incorrect Birth Chart Data
Uses non-standard settings leading to wrong planetary placements. Retrograde planets ignored in interpretations.


2. The Pattern — Negative Reviews & Complaints
The Pattern has a passionate user base but has alienated them through aggressive monetization and a decline in content quality:

THE PATTERN
Paywalling Previously Free Content
Moved content behind subscription without adding new value. Users say it's 'literally the same info but now you can't access it without paying monthly.' Triggered mass one-star reviews.
No Astrological Context
Gives personality insights without saying which planets or aspects caused them. Intermediate/advanced users frustrated by missing 'cause and effect' explanation.
No Birth Chart Display
Doesn't show users their actual birth chart. Users who want to learn astrology have nowhere to start.
Negative Focus Without Solutions
Repeatedly highlights unhealthy patterns without offering advice on how to change them. Feels like a 'diagnosis with no treatment'.
Confusing UI After Updates
Redesigns have repeatedly scrambled navigation. Cycles no longer list upcoming events, everything crammed on one page.
Removed Journaling / Community
Beloved journaling and shared experiences features were removed. Users still requesting them years later.
AI Chat Feels Unsafe
New AI chat feature criticized for being dishonest about being AI. Users concerned about 'AI-induced psychosis' with astrology-wrapped chatbots.
Pricing Model Friction
Only quarterly/annual subscription available for a long time — no monthly option. Felt like a cash grab.
Login & Tech Bugs
Multiple users reported hours wasted trying to sign up due to SMS verification failures and broken email links.


3. Sanctuary — Negative Reviews & Complaints
Sanctuary differentiates with live astrologer access, but the execution has significant gaps:

SANCTUARY
Fake / Rushed Live Readings
Readers contacted users within 3 minutes of a reading being 'performed', impossible for a real reading. Readings felt scripted and generic.
Reading Duration Not Disclosed
Users discover the reading is only 15 minutes AFTER paying. No transparency on length or value upfront.
Inaccurate Birth Chart Data
Multiple users report placements changing between app versions, contradicting every other astrology tool they use.
No Reader Testimonials
Advisor profiles lack client reviews or testimonials, making it hard to trust or choose a reader.
Generic Guides for Paying Members
Paid subscription guides are 'very generic' — if you know any astrology, you won't learn anything new.
Poor Customer Support
Technical glitches with no easy support channel. Users lose paid readings with no way to recover them.
Can't Look Up Non-Users Easily
Users want to look up charts for friends who haven't downloaded the app. Feature is paywalled or nonexistent.
Push to Upsell Readings
App constantly nudges users toward buying paid readings, making the free experience feel like a sales funnel.


4. TimePassages — Negative Reviews & Complaints
TimePassages is respected for accuracy but is functionally aging poorly:

TIMEPASSAGES
Dated, Cluttered UI
Interface looks and feels stuck in 2012. Overwhelming for beginners. Not optimized for modern iPhone screen sizes.
Data Loss / No Cloud Sync
Users have lost all purchased charts with no backup. Customer support told them to 'wipe their phone'. Purchases non-recoverable.
Pay-Per-Chart Model Frustrating
Paying $0.99 per chart for friends/family adds up. No subscription or bundle option. Feels nickel-and-dime.
Appears Abandoned
No meaningful updates in years. Users suspect the team is just 'collecting money on existing users' without developing further.
No Consultation Feature
Purely self-service. No live astrologer access. Users must interpret complex information entirely on their own.
GPS/Location Bugs
Location detection fails regularly, affecting chart accuracy. Whole Signs house system produces incorrect results.
Poor Navigation / No State Save
Leaves you at the main screen every time you re-open the app. No memory of where you were in the content.


5. Recurring Themes Across ALL Apps
The following complaints appear in reviews across every competitor — these are the biggest universal pain points in the market:

The 8 Universal Complaints
💸 Monetization Betrayal: Aggressive paywalling of previously free features, with no additional value added
😰 Negativity Without Solutions: Content that is emotionally negative, anxiety-inducing, or purely diagnostic without offering guidance
🤖 Generic / Repetitive Content: Readings that feel AI-generated, templated, and repetitive day after day
📉 Astrological Inaccuracy: No professional astrologers involved; house systems and chart settings that professionals reject
🎓 No Educational Layer: Deep insights given with no explanation of which planet/aspect caused them — users can't learn
🤝 Missing Community & Personal Tools: Journaling, community, and social features removed or never built
🛠 Technical Reliability Failures: Broken login, lost data, no cloud backup, poor customer support
💬 Transparency Issues: Unclear pricing, hidden subscription lengths, forced upsells in-app
